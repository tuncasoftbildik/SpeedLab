Frequently Asked Questions

How does the speed test work?

SpeedLab measures download, upload, ping and jitter using Cloudflare's global CDN for accurate, location-aware results.

What does SpeedLab Pro include?

Pro removes ads and unlocks unlimited test history, the WiFi signal map, CSV/PDF export, and the home screen widget. It's available as a monthly or yearly auto-renewable subscription.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → select SpeedLab Pro. From there you can change your plan or cancel. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

How do I restore a purchase?

In SpeedLab, open Settings and tap Restore Purchases, or use the Restore button on the Pro upgrade screen.

Does SpeedLab collect my personal data?

SpeedLab does not collect personally identifiable information. Test history is stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
